Deployed Docker Containers

Sonarr

Automated TV series downloader & organizer. Monitors feeds and sends new episodes to your torrent client.

Radarr

Movie-focused counterpart to Sonarr. Tracks upcoming releases and fetches them automatically with your downloader.

Portainer

Lightweight management UI for Docker. Provides a visual dashboard for containers, images, networks, and volumes.

qBittorrent

User-friendly, open-source BitTorrent client that handles all your torrent downloads, watched by Sonarr/Radarr.

Jackett

Acts as a proxy for torrent indexers. Translates tracker queries into a compatible format for Sonarr/Radarr.

Netdata

Real-time performance monitoring. Visualizes CPU, memory, disk, network, and container metrics at a glance.

Tautulli

Plex monitoring & analytics. Tracks user activity, notifications, and streaming stats for your Plex server.

Nextcloud

Self-hosted file sync & share, cloud storage, calendars, contacts, and more, all under your control.
